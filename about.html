About

Hi, my name is Steven

I make fitbit clock faces.

I've been doing so for almost (as of May 2020) 3 months now. I started back in February when I got my first fitbit, the Fitbit Versa 2, and since then, I have made and published for review almost 12 clocks. My first clock was published back on March 12th, and it was free. Despite being very flawed and not being the best clock, it got over 44 ratings with an average of 4.4 stars, which I found a success. I then proceeded to develop more clocks and my second one got published almost a month later after the numerous rejections. It got a total of 3 ratings (unsurprising, it was a paid app) with an average 5.0 stars. This of course, freaked me out. Even though it only got a couple purchases, I was excited to start publishing the next couple. And that's where we are today. Working hard on school but in the mean time, developing crazy new clockfaces.
